Gains4Devs

Develop yourself.

The ultimate workout app designed specifically for time-strapped developers who want to stay fit and healthy despite their busy schedules.

Use this app to generate a list of random workouts to target specific muscle groups you want to work. Created to provide quick ideas to break up your routine, and keep you motivated!

Instructions:

  • 1. Pick the muscle groups you want to workout.
  • 2. Choose a number between 1-10 to multiply the number of workouts returned.
  • 3. Complete your workout!
